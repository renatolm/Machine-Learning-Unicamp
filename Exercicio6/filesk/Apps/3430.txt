
Today, Yahoo has announced a new Flickr experience powered by Oculus for Samsung Gear VR. The team says that over the years, users have uploaded “tens of thousands” of 360° photos and you’ll now be able to view them in a full-immersive VR experience.
It’s a welcomed addition to the suite of apps that are making Samsung’s Gear VR fly off of the shelves this holiday season.
